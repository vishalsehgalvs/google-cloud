# 21-Day Deep Dive Schedule

Use this schedule for full-coverage mastery.

## Week 1: Foundation and control plane

Day 1:

- Cloud models, global infra, business value
- Outcome: explain IaaS/PaaS/serverless/SaaS from memory

Day 2:

- Resource hierarchy and projects
- Outcome: explain policy inheritance and project role

Day 3:

- IAM roles, members, policies
- Outcome: solve access questions with least-privilege logic

Day 4:

- Service accounts and IAM best practices
- Outcome: separate human identity vs workload identity correctly

Day 5:

- Billing, budgets, alerts, reports, quotas
- Outcome: solve cost-control scenarios quickly

Day 6:

- Compute service selection
- Outcome: choose right compute service by scenario in under 30 seconds

Day 7:

- Weekly review and mixed quiz drill

## Week 2: Data, storage, networking, operations

Day 8:

- Storage service model and class selection

Day 9:

- Database choice: Cloud SQL, Spanner, Firestore, Bigtable, Memorystore

Day 10:

- Data services: BigQuery, Dataflow, Dataproc, Dataprep

Day 11:

- Networking fundamentals: VPC, subnets, routes, firewall, IP

Day 12:

- Delivery services: DNS, CDN, load balancing

Day 13:

- Connectivity options and architecture-level tradeoffs

Day 14:

- Observability stack and reliability framing

## Week 3: Integration, speed, and exam readiness

Day 15:

- AI and business transformation framing

Day 16:

- Terraform and automation at decision level

Day 17:

- Scenario set A: service selection only

Day 18:

- Scenario set B: security and cost controls

Day 19:

- Scenario set C: networking and reliability

Day 20:

- Full mixed mock-style revision pass

Day 21:

- Final recall, weak-area patching, low-stress review

## Daily format

- 40 min: primary read
- 20 min: notes in your own words
- 20 min: scenario practice
- 10 min: rapid recall
