# Combined Study Dashboard (Super Crisp + Deep Dive)

Use this as your single control panel.

## Tracks Available

- Super Crisp Track: `practitioner_super_crisp/`
- Deep Dive Track: `practitioner_deep_dive_path/`

## How To Decide Which Track Today

Choose Super Crisp if:

- you have less than 60 minutes
- you need quick recall refresh
- you feel overloaded and want confidence fast

Choose Deep Dive if:

- you have 90+ minutes
- you want full conceptual clarity
- you are weak in scenario-based questions

Recommended blend:

- 70% deep dive + 30% super crisp until final week
- 40% deep dive + 60% super crisp in final week

## Daily Session Template

## A) 45-minute day (busy day)

1. Read `practitioner_super_crisp/07_master_standalone_revision_sheet.md` (20 min)
2. Read `practitioner_super_crisp/02_service_choice_in_30_seconds.md` (10 min)
3. Do 5 oral scenario answers from `practitioner_deep_dive_path/07_hands_on_and_scenario_drills.md` (15 min)

## B) 90-minute day (standard day)

1. Read one deep-dive domain file (35 min)
2. Write your own summary in 10 bullets (15 min)
3. Solve scenario drills (25 min)
4. End with super-crisp recall (15 min)

## C) 150-minute day (high-intensity day)

1. Deep-dive domain study (45 min)
2. Scenario drill workbook (40 min)
3. Weak-topic correction pass (30 min)
4. Super-crisp fast recall (20 min)
5. 15-minute self-test oral recap (15 min)
